Tic Tac Toe

"Strategize, compete, and claim victory in the timeless battle of Tic Tac Toe."

Ready to outmaneuver your opponent and emerge triumphant in Tic Tac Toe? Sharpen your tactics, anticipate their every move, and embark on the ultimate journey to victory! Play now and become the master of the grid!

  • Studios: By Arjun Singh Rajput
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Brain-Storming

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Achieve a winning pattern by placing three of your symbols (X or O) in a row, column, or diagonal on the game board.

Controls:

  • Click on an empty cell on the game board to place your symbol (X or O) in that position.

Gameplay:

  • Both Player 1 and Player 2 will Enter their name And start the Game.
  • Player 1 starts the game by placing their symbol (X) on an empty cell.
  • Player 2 (or the computer in single-player mode) then places their symbol (O) on an empty cell.
  • Players take turns placing their symbols until one achieves a winning pattern or the board is filled, resulting in a draw.
