Space Invaders

"Defend Earth, Defeat Invaders, Save the Galaxy!"

Are you ready to defend Earth from the alien menace? Gather your courage, take aim, and prepare for the ultimate showdown in Space Invaders. Play now and become the hero that Earth desperately needs!

  • Studios: By Garvit Kedia
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Adventure

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Destroy all incoming alien invaders while avoiding their attacks. Protect Earth at all costs!

Controls:

  • Use the left and right arrow keys to move your spaceship horizontally across the bottom of the screen.
  • Press the space bar to fire bullets and obliterate the alien invaders.

Gameplay:

  • The game starts with a fleet of alien invaders descending from the top of the screen towards Earth.
  • Your spaceship is stationed at the bottom, poised to defend against the incoming threat.
  • Move your spaceship left or right to align your shots with the advancing aliens.
  • Fire bullets to destroy the aliens. Each successful hit earns you points.
