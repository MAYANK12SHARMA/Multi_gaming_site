Flappy Bird

"Fly into Fun: Flap your way through obstacles in this addictive avian adventure!"

Dive into the addictive world of Flappy Bird! Tap to flap your wings and navigate through treacherous pipes. With simple controls and endless challenges, this game will keep you hooked for hours. Can you beat your high score and become the ultimate flapper?

  • Studios: By Parth Garg
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Adventure

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Guide the bird through the gaps between pipes without hitting them.

Controls:

  • Tap the Space Bar to make the bird flap its wings and ascend.
  • Release Space Bar to let the bird descend.

Gameplay:

  • Start the game by tapping the screen to make the bird fly.
  • Guide the bird through openings between pipes by tapping to make it flap its wings.
  • Avoid colliding with the pipes or the ground, as this will end the game.
  • Keep tapping to maintain the bird's altitude and navigate through the obstacles.
  • Challenge yourself to beat your high score and see how far you can flap!
