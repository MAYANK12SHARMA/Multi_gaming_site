Sprite Flight

"Unleash the power of the canine warrior! Navigate perilous obstacles, defeat enemies, and prove your prowess in Flight - the ultimate test of doggy dexterity!"

Prepare to embark on an epic adventure with Flight! Take control of a fearless canine warrior as you dash through challenging landscapes, facing off against formidable foes. With lightning-fast reflexes and strategic maneuvers, you must overcome obstacles and vanquish enemies to earn points and beat the clock. Harness the fury of Canis Rage, execute daring Dive Attacks, and race against time to achieve victory. Are you ready to lead your furry friend to glory in Flight?

  • Studios: By Arjun Singh Rajput
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Adventure

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Defeat enemies and earn a minimum of 25 points within the time limit of 35 seconds.

Controls:

  • Use the 'W', 'A', 'S', 'D' keys to move the dog character.
  • Press 'J' to enable Canis Rage and unleash powerful attacks on enemies.
  • Perform a Dive Attack by pressing 'W' + 'S' keys simultaneously.

Gameplay:

  • Control the dog character to navigate through the game environment.
  • Defeat enemies by attacking them using various moves and abilities.
  • Collect points by defeating enemies, and aim to achieve a minimum of 25 points.
  • Keep an eye on the timer and complete the objective within the time limit of 35 seconds.
  • Utilize special abilities like Canis Rage and Dive Attack strategically to overcome challenges and secure victory!
