Whack A Mole

"Watch out for surprises and whack those moles! Can you keep up with the fast-paced fun?"

Dive into the thrilling world of Whack-a-Mole! Keep your reflexes sharp as you watch for pesky moles popping up from their holes. But beware of unexpected obstacles! With its fast-paced action and addictive gameplay, this classic arcade game will keep you entertained for hours. Get ready to test your agility and become the ultimate mole whacker!

  • Studios: By Parth Garg
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Comedy

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Watch and click on the moles as they pop out of their holes while avoiding dangerous objects.

Controls:

  • Use the mouse cursor to click on the moles as they appear.
  • Avoid clicking on any other objects that may pop out alongside the moles.

Gameplay:

  • Keep your eyes peeled for moles popping out of their holes.
  • Click on the moles quickly and accurately before they disappear.
  • Avoid clicking on any other objects, as this may result in penalties or loss of points.
  • Stay vigilant and react swiftly to watch as many moles as possible within the time limit.
  • Test your reflexes and aim for the highest score!
