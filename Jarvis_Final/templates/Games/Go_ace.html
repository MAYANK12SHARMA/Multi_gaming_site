Go For Ace

"Deal yourself into a world of excitement and strategy with our captivating card game!"

Ready to deal, bluff, and conquer the table? Gear up, strategize your plays, and embark on the ultimate card-playing journey. Unleash your inner tactician and dominate the game now!

  • Studios: By Garvit Kedia
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre:Mystery, Luck

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Find and select the Ace of Spades from the displayed cards after they've been shuffled.

Controls:

  • Click or tap on the card you believe is the Ace of Spades after the shuffle.

Gameplay:

  • Start the game with four cards displayed, one of which is the Ace of Spades.
  • After a shuffle, the cards are rearranged, obscuring the location of the Ace of Spades.
  • Your task is to remember or discern the position of the Ace of Spades and click on it.
  • Choose wisely to uncover the Ace of Spades and advance to the next level or win!
