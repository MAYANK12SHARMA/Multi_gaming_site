Brick Breaker

Break, bounce, and conquer your way through endless levels of excitement in Brick Breaker!

Ready to smash, bounce, and rise to the top of the leaderboard? Gear up, hone your reflexes, and embark on the ultimate brick-breaking journey. Unleash your inner demolisher and conquer the challenge now!

  • Studios: By Vaishvik Sharma
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Adventure

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Destroy all the bricks on the screen using the ball and paddle.

Controls:

  • Use the left and right arrow keys or A and D keys to move the paddle horizontally.
  • Press the space bar to launch the ball and start the game.

Gameplay:

  • Control the paddle to bounce the ball and prevent it from falling below the paddle.
  • Hit the ball with the paddle to destroy the bricks on the screen.
  • Each brick destroyed earns points.
  • Avoid letting the ball fall off the bottom of the screen.
  • Break all the bricks to complete the level and advance to the next.
