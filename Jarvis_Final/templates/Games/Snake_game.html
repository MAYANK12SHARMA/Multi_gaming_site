Snake Game

"Slither, evade, and grow your way to the top in the classic Snake game!"

Ready to slither, devour, and dominate the leaderboard? Gear up, strategize your moves, and embark on the ultimate journey in Snake. Unleash your inner serpent and conquer the challenge now!

  • Studios: By Vaishvik Sharma
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Adventure

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Control the snake to consume as many balls as possible without colliding with the walls or itself.

Controls:

  • Use the arrow keys to change the direction of the snake's movement.

Gameplay:

  • Guide the snake to move around the game board.
  • As the snake consumes balls, it grows longer.
  • Avoid colliding with the walls or the snake's own body.
  • Keep consuming balls to score points and increase the snake's length.
  • Survive as long as possible and aim for the highest score!
