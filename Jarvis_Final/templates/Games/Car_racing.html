Car Racing

"Rev up your engines and leave the competition in the dust! Experience the thrill of high-speed racing like never before!"

Experience the adrenaline-pumping excitement of our car racing game! Immerse yourself in stunning graphics, realistic physics, and intense competition as you race against the clock and rival drivers. With customizable cars and challenging tracks, every race is a test of skill and speed.

  • Studios: By Garvit Kedia
  • Date Launched: Feb, 2024
  • Status: Airing
  • Genre: Action, Racing, Sports

Instructions and Rules

Objective:

  • Race against the computer-controlled car and be the first to cross the finish line.

Controls:

  • Use the arrow keys to accelerate, steer left, and steer right.

Gameplay:

  • Start the race from the starting line alongside the computer-controlled car.
  • Accelerate your car using the arrow keys to gain speed and maintain momentum.
  • Steer left and right to navigate through twists, turns, and obstacles on the track.
  • Keep an eye on your opponent and strategically use power-ups to gain an advantage.
  • Race with skill and precision to outmaneuver the computer car and secure victory!
